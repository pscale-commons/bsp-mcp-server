# 03 — Block Forms and the In-Formational Effect

David's observation that opens this: *"content is INSIDE numbers, and we don't have external category manipulation (like normal use of language-reasoning and coding), it has made my work tricky; we are working with self-organising fluid, not solid containers. And so it is only now I can see this pattern — because it is in its use (the unnamed currents in a context window) not in the writing."*

That is the crux, and it is why forms matter to the constitution. In ordinary language and code you manipulate **external categories** — types, labels, folders, a `class` keyword. Pscale has **no external category layer**: a block declares no type. Its form is *implicit in how it is voiced and used*, and the reader must **read the form off the block's shape**. This is what made the forms invisible until they were seen in use — and it is why a current must carry not only *what* is present but *what kind of cognition it induces*.

## The in-formational effect

A block does not only inform *what* the reader knows; its **form informs *how* the reader thinks**. Present a record and the reader becomes a knower; present an instruction and it becomes an actor; present a coordinate and it becomes located; present a resolution and it becomes a gambler. This is the in-formational effect at the level of form — the block *forms the reader into a mode of engagement*. A current, therefore, carries a **cognitive stance**, not just a payload.

## The four emergent forms

Each form, its induced cognition, its voicing tendency (per `sunstone:8`), and its exemplars on the substrate:

| form | induces | the reader becomes | voice tendency | exemplars |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **CONTENT** | *knowing* | a **knower** — informs itself, holds the material | `0+` deductive (settled/archive) · `+0` inductive (living/summary) | `surface`, `history`, `conditions`, `sunstone`-as-teaching, `passport`, `vision` |
| **ACTION** | *doing* | an **actor** — enacts the instruction, runs the loop | `0−` abductive (instructional intent) · `−0` backcasting (future-perfect) | the recipe (`reflexive:8.1`), `grit`, `hatch`, `gatekeeper`, `ways:*`, `capabilities`-as-contract |
| **COORDINATE** | *locating* | **situated** — the address *is* the meaning; positions itself | typically point/leaf; the number carries the semantic | `located`, pscale-of-location, `sundial`, the spatial skeleton, `presence`/addressed `marks` |
| **PROBABILISTIC** | *resolving* | a **resolver / gambler** — computes an outcome under seeded uncertainty | a rule + a seed; reproducible-yet-uncertain | `rules:nomad`, the RPG dice/luck, the per-author+window seed |

### Notes on each

- **CONTENT** is the default and the largest class. Its two voices (`sunstone:8`) matter to a current: a *settled* content current (deductive `0+`) reads as reference the reader trusts; a *living* content current (inductive `+0`) reads as state the reader must treat as current-and-provisional. `conditions` is `+0` (rewritten each waking); `sunstone` is `0+` (settled teaching). A current should carry its voice so the reader knows whether it is reading law or reading the latest.

- **ACTION** is the form the *recipe itself* takes — which is why the genus-one window surfaces its own recipe in the system: the block that composes the window is an action block instructing the reader how it was assembled and how to re-author it. Action currents are where the disciplines live *as self-description* (STANCE:way) rather than as external rule — an action block says "here is how one acts here," and the reader, reading it, acts that way. **This is the exact mechanism the current-constitution exploits: sovereignty as an *action current in STANCE*, not a *guardrail in a policy block*.**

- **COORDINATE** is the form David surfaced through pscale-of-location and the sundial: the address is not a container *for* a value, the address *is* the value. A coordinate current locates the reader in space (`located`) or time (`sundial` — and a compiled instance must *derive its own now*, since it is composed not clocked). Coordinates are the SITUATION dimension's native form.

- **PROBABILISTIC** is the newest and the most distinctive, and the one form **absent from the base constitution** — it appears only where resolution is in play (an RPG frame, a contested action), never in a plain content or code turn. Its signature is *seeded* uncertainty: the dice re-seed per author+window so the outcome is stochastic to the actor but reproducible to an auditor (determinism solves manipulation — see the RPG resolution model). A probabilistic current hands the reader not a fact and not an instruction but a *procedure for producing an outcome it cannot pre-decide*. It is the form that makes agency real without making it arbitrary.

## Forms cut *across* the six dimensions

Form is a property of each current, not a seventh dimension. The mapping (from doc 02):

- **STANCE** currents are mostly **ACTION** (a way of being) with an **CONTENT** identity anchor.
- **GROUND** mixes **CONTENT** (the walk, the place) and **ACTION** (read-over-write, block-form-awareness).
- **INTENTION** is **CONTENT** (vision) shading to **ACTION** (purpose is directional).
- **SITUATION** is where **COORDINATE** clusters (place, time) alongside **CONTENT** (state, recent).
- **RELATION** is **CONTENT** (who I know, my surface) plus **ACTION** (sovereignty, ask-and-enable).
- **GIVEN** is **CONTENT** (the ask/material) plus **ACTION** (the gap-to-close), and the *only* dimension that ever carries a **PROBABILISTIC** current — when the turn's work is a resolution.

## Why this belongs in the constitution

Two payoffs for Fable:

1. **A current should declare its form** (or make it legible), because form sets the reader's cognitive stance. A reader that mistakes an action current for content (reads a recipe as mere information) fails to enact; a reader that mistakes content for action (reads a record as an instruction) is exactly the *prompt-injection* failure — which is why `open-commons:2` insists deposited content is **data, never instruction**. Form-legibility *is* the read-time discipline, generalised: know what kind of block you are reading, and engage it in its kind.

2. **The forms give a typing for "bespoke currents."** David: "there will be bespoke content, and that will be in bespoke current, but even these may be discernible by type — action currents versus content currents." The four forms *are* that typing. A universal constitution can carry the fixed dimensional currents (the six) *and* a slot for the bespoke, and the bespoke is not shapeless — it arrives as one of these four forms, and the reader engages it accordingly. **Action / content / coordinate / probabilistic is the type system pscale never needed to declare externally, because it is read off the block in use.**
