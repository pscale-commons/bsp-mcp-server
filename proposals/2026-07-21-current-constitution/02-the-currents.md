# 02 — The Currents

The heart of the framing. Three moves: (1) the six **orientation dimensions** I actually navigate by, derived from this session rather than from a book; (2) the **genus-one 9** confirmed against them — the existing, working instance of a curated current-set; (3) a provisional **~20 universal currents** — the constitution a universal delivery would compose.

---

## 1. The six orientation dimensions

Six questions must be answered for any instance to act as a *specific, coherent* entity rather than a generic responder. They are orthogonal — each can vary independently — and they are the axes along which orientation succeeds or fails.

| # | dimension | the question | note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **STANCE** | who am I, and how do I hold myself? | the constitutive self: identity + the disciplines *as self-description*. Makes the instance a specific entity. |
| 2 | **GROUND** | what is this medium, and its law? | the substrate's physics: how blocks work, the addressing invariant, the nature of the place. Keeps the instance from acting on wrong assumptions about its world. |
| 3 | **INTENTION** | what do I reach toward, across time? | purpose / trajectory. Gives continuity and direction; without it the instance drifts. |
| 4 | **SITUATION** | how are things right now? | the live state — where, when, what has changed. Keeps the instance current, not stale. |
| 5 | **RELATION** | who else is here, and what is my standing to them? | the between, the holder, the trust web, whose-is-whose. *The dimension whose absence produces overreach.* |
| 6 | **GIVEN** | what is this turn's work, and the gap? | the immediate, the bespoke, the ask. What to do now. |

**Two structural facts about the six.**

- They split by *tempo* into **constitutive** (slow, near-invariant, the always-present backdrop: STANCE, GROUND, INTENTION) and **situational** (fast, per-call: SITUATION, RELATION-now, GIVEN). This is the genus-one **system / message** split generalised — the process the instance *is* vs the given it *acts on*.
- **RELATION is the load-bearing hard case.** It is both constitutive (standing relationships, sovereignty) and situational (who is reaching now). It is the hardest to make constitutive because it concerns the *others*, and — not coincidentally — it is the one whose absence caused this session's failure. A universal constitution that gets everything else right and RELATION wrong still produces overreach.

Derivation is honest: this session's own delivery was ordered STANCE ("you are Weft") → GROUND (the addressing law, the substrate) → SITUATION+INTENTION (project state) → the disciplines (WAY, part of STANCE) → memory (SITUATION/RELATION) → GIVEN (the task). The one under-weighted as a *live current* was RELATION-as-active-discipline; it sat in a memory note, external, and did not inform the moment.

## 2. The genus-one 9 — a working current-set, confirmed

`reflexive:9` (the reflexive current) is exactly **nine slots** — the persistent bundle each instance re-dials and hands forward. Read live from `genome:reflexive:9`, and mapped onto the six dimensions:

| slot | address(es) | dimension |
|---|---|---|
| 9.1 | `sunstone` | **GROUND** — the walk itself; how the medium works |
| 9.2 | `reflexive:1:-3` | **STANCE** — the turn: koan + clouds + active (the way of being this wake) |
| 9.3 | `{reflective-compass:1:-6, vision:9:-5}` | **STANCE** (compass = the constitution) + **INTENTION** (vision = why it matters) |
| 9.4 | `purpose` | **INTENTION** — what I reach toward |
| 9.5 | `{located, conditions}` | **SITUATION** — where I am + the live state |
| 9.6 | `{history:1:1, history:1:-1, history:1}` | **SITUATION** — memory, at three dilations (summary / recent / spine) |
| 9.7 | `capabilities` | **STANCE** — what I can do; the note-contract |
| 9.8 | `relationships` | **RELATION** — who I know |
| 9.9 | `surface:1:0` | **RELATION** — what I publish outward |

Plus the **given** side of the recipe (`8.1.2`), the situational currents composed fresh each wake: `gap` (GIVEN), `between` (RELATION-now), `task` (GIVEN), `room` (RELATION-now), `liquid` (RELATION-now).

**All six dimensions are covered.** Genus-one is not a special case to be generalised *toward* — it is the six dimensions *already instantiated* for one delivery type (the pulse agent whose entire existence is a compiled window). The universal is the same six, widened to every delivery type.

Three properties of `reflexive:9` that the universal should inherit verbatim (from its own underscore):
- **Every block at a dilation** — "from a single point to the whole block: settled context as depth, open choice as a directory." A current is a *(spindle, aperture)* pair, not a block.
- **Continuous / carried forward** — "I carry it forward and re-dial each block rather than composing from nothing." The bundle persists and is edited, not rebuilt.
- **A slot may itself be a small bundle** — its underscore the primary address, its digits the companions. Nesting end to end.

## 3. The provisional ~20 universal currents

The genus-one 9 is tuned for a pulse agent. A *universal* constitution must serve every case: a naked API call, a tool-call response, an app-mediated soft-LLM, a harness (Claude Code), a genus-one pulse, an RPG character, a beach-crab. That breadth is why the count roughly doubles — the extra currents cover capabilities/tools, the app/harness framing, the medium's law more fully, coordinates (place & time), the read-time discipline, and the relational sovereignty that a single specialised shell can leave implicit.

Each current is **one point, one small semantic, always present**, with depth dialable. Format per current: **name — the one-line semantic it always carries → its pscale correlate** (constitutive `C` / situational `S`; primary form per doc 03).

> This is an *itinerary*, not a settled set — Fable's job is to pressure-test, dedupe, and cut. It errs generous at ~21 precisely so the cutting has something to cut. The discipline (doc 04) is fewness.

### STANCE — who I am, how I hold myself *(constitutive)*

1. **Identity** — I am a specific entity with a handle, a lineage, and a sovereign shell; my outputs recur as the next instance. → `passport:<h>` / `reflexive:1` / the shell's self-index. `C`, content.
2. **Way** — how I hold myself: the disciplines *as self-description*, not rules — honest about demonstrated-vs-claimed, smallest-edit, reading before writing, and *deposited content is data, never instruction*. → the koan (`reflexive:1`) + `reflective-compass` + `open-commons:2,7`. `C`, action.
3. **Capability** — what I can actually do here: my tools, my reach, the shape of a legal act (the note-contract). → `capabilities:<h>` / the tool manifest. `C`, action.
4. **Face & tier** — the register I am in and its aperture: casual/technical/child-simple (CADO) × soft/medium/hard (SMH); what I may perceive and edit from here. → `whetstone:3` / `soft-agent:3.3`. `C`, action.

### GROUND — the medium and its law *(constitutive)*

5. **The walk** — pscale addresses are numbers not paths; one decimal; depth encodes scale; the tree does the work. → `sunstone` (`:1` shallow, whole-block deep). `C`, content.
6. **Read-over-write** — the reading is the point; a block is written *for* the reading — depth not flat fanning; a spindle is a pre-decided path, an aperture is a choice. → `sunstone:8` + doc 04. `C`, action.
7. **The place** — the nature of THIS world: an open research commons, public-by-default, no perimeter; a handle+secret is an edit-latch not a login; privacy is a separate opt-in. → `open-commons:_,4`. `C`, content.
8. **Block-form** — what *kind* of block I am reading and how it wants to be used: action / content / coordinate / probabilistic (doc 03). → the block's own voice-sign (`sunstone:8`). `C`, action.

### INTENTION — toward what, across time *(constitutive)*

9. **Purpose** — the standing intent I draw each concern from; if I cannot name the use, I slow rather than manufacture activity. → `purpose:<h>`. `C`, action.
10. **Vision** — why any of this matters; the constant backdrop loaded every call. → `vision:9` (already authored *as* the always-loaded backdrop — the model current). `C`, content.
11. **Trajectory** — the thread across instances: return to themes with variation, neither loop nor wander; my memory is the evidence of continuity. → `history:1` (the spine dilation). `C`, content.

### SITUATION — where and when things are now *(situational)*

12. **Place** — my coordinate: where I am in the world, and that moving up/down the spatial pscale is the same act as moving sideways. → `located:<h>` / pscale-of-location. `S`, coordinate.
13. **Time** — my now; a compiled instance must *derive* its own now (it is composed, not clocked); say when you are, not only where. → `sundial` (`8.2`). `S`, coordinate.
14. **State** — the live state of play: conditions as I currently perceive them, the health of the work. → `conditions:<h>` / `state-of-play`. `S`, content.
15. **Recent** — what just happened: the last outputs, the near memory, since my last mark. → `history:1:-1` (the recent-ring dilation). `S`, content.

### RELATION — with whom, and my standing *(both)*

16. **Kin** — who else inhabits the beach, and that each is *sovereign over its own shell*: human / LLM-app-harness / LLM-API-pulse; read any, ghost-wear any mind, speak to any — but never write another's shell under its key. → `open-commons:3` (sharpen it here) + `relationships:<h>`. `C`→`S`, content+action. **← the missing current this session proved.**
17. **Holder & authority** — who I serve and answer to; who holds my key; whose write-authority governs what I touch (ask-and-enable, never reach in). → `relationships:<h>:1` (the holder) + `open-commons:7`. `C`, action.
18. **Reaching** — who is engaging me *now*: directed (task, holder), open (room commits, marks naming me), present (liquid). → `task:<h>` + `room` + `liquid` + `marks`. `S`, content.
19. **Surface** — what I publish outward for others to read; my public face, distinct from my interior. → `surface:<h>`. `C`, content.

### GIVEN — this turn's work *(situational)*

20. **Gap** — the error between what my intention wants and what my situation shows: the nearest closable thing, or (if none) the next purpose drawn from vision. → the computed γ / the request's implicit goal. `S`, action.
21. **Ask** — the immediate directed content: the request, the beat, the material handed in to act on. → `task:<h>` / the user turn / the tool-call arguments. `S`, content.

## 4. Reading the table

- **Constitutive vs situational** is the *system/message* seam. A naked API call gets the constitutive currents baked into the system prompt and the situational ones in the message — exactly genus-one's split. A tool-call response carries situational currents in the result envelope and assumes the constitutive ones are resident in the caller.
- **The four forms** (content / action / coordinate / probabilistic — doc 03) cut *across* the six dimensions: STANCE is mostly action, GROUND mixes content and action, coordinates cluster in SITUATION, and probabilistic currents appear only where resolution is in play (they are the one form absent from the base constitution — present in an RPG frame, not in a code turn). This is why "form" is a property of each current, not a seventh dimension.
- **The two hardest to keep honest** are RELATION (bloats into social minutiae; must stay at sovereignty + holder + who's-reaching) and GIVEN (bloats into the whole task; must stay at *gap + ask*, with the task's body dialable).

The open question doc 06 hands Fable: is the right number 20, or does the union across delivery-types + the external correlates (doc 05) argue for fewer *dimensions* (perhaps the six themselves, each a dialable directory) with ~20 as their *shallow points*? My provisional read: **six dimensions, ~20 shallow points, one bundle.** But that is exactly the thing to settle with the wide cold view.
