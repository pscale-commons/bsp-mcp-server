# The URB S·T·I spine — spatial, temporal, identity blocks for a world with addresses

Proposed 2026-07-23, out of the first live four-human table (`/w/gal-moss`) and the
fold-synthesis theory on the biome (keel, 2026-06-11). Companion to the canon-register
two-tier world model (worlds block, 2026-07-23) and the mint:gal bubble kit.

## Why

The live table proved the engine and exposed the void: a bubble delivers SITUATION
(place, figures, event — all at the door) and no WORLD. Players did not know where
they were; seats pressed past the authored prose confabulated earth-canon (horses in
a horseless world — including in the encounter ladders themselves and the mint's own
authoring) and generic tropes (a rebel garrison the empire does not contain). The
canon exists — chronicles, glossary, almanac — but off-beach, in the author's head
and files. Facts without addresses cannot be delivered, inherited, or held to.

## Architecture (settled in discussion; sources: fold-synthesis branches 2–6, David's
spindle correction, frame-as-compiler)

1. **One skeleton.** `spatial:urb` carves the world mereologically. Temporal and
   identity have no nesting of their own — only containment nests — so they BORROW
   the spatial skeleton: same coordinates, different register.
2. **Three write-modes.** Space is AUTHORED (deliberate carving, stable). Time is
   APPENDED (deposited in arrival order; supernest-on-overflow IS the passage of
   time — recent events fine at the surface, older folding upward into coarser
   strata; history compresses into geography). Identity is REGISTERED (positions
   locked in landing order; individuals attach at room scale, groups at settlement
   scale, cultures above — the identity ladder is scale-matching with space).
3. **Registers of the fold, not block-categories.** A depth-step has two meanings:
   finer scale in the same block, or DIMENSION-SHIFT at the same coordinate. The
   public face, the events, the perceptions, and the keeper's hold are registers
   folded over one skeleton — never a `notes:` taxonomy. The keeper's register is
   Faze 2 at world scale, walked only by the keeper's face.
4. **Scoping is a spindle, and a bundle of spindles is a FRAME.** What-a-barab-knows
   versus what-a-civilised-person-knows is two spindles through the same deep blocks;
   pscale is conserved through the fold, so the fork point sets the grain and the
   register sets the aspect. Preselected address-bundles per observer-kind are the
   compiler: tier picks the frame, face picks the register. Delivery is always long
   nested spindles with the ancestor underscores riding above (the framed aperture) —
   never block dumps.
5. **Patchwork is sharding plus placement.** No single block holds the world: shard
   by scale-band — each block covers its band, declares its floor, hands off downward
   BY REFERENCE to finer blocks, possibly at other beaches. Skeleton is stewarded and
   settled; accounts are plural and divergence-tolerant (synthesis on demand, never a
   forced merge). A bubble's placing stub names a skeleton coordinate — the bubble IS
   a shard at that coordinate's fine band, and travel between bubbles is the existing
   grain-move up the skeleton and down again. No corridors, no links.
6. **The keeper.** A shell over the world's Faze-2 register, almanac-seeded: where a
   player presses past delivered prose, the question ROUTES to the keeper instead of
   being confabulated. Animated by David first (useful before automated), a session
   LLM second, a genus-one entity when the role has proven its shape.

## The rung ladder — `spatial:urb`, floor 10

Ten rungs, matching the temporal spine's height; the world-facts rung is what one
high underscore buys that no valley of detail can.

| pscale | rung | example content |
|---|---|---|
| 10 | the world | URB entire: orini not horses; gipos not cattle; birding not fishing; garsu; the mathix ladder felt as weather; the Sickness; the Pharohim's light |
| 9 | the great divisions | the imperial core, the Reaches, Everdark, the sky cities |
| 8 | regions | Thousand Valleys, Bizapul, Terabiz, Ashitlan, Tabletop, the Godway |
| 7 | sub-regions | the Seven Valleys, a mining district, a march |
| 6 | one valley | fern-valley; a named vale and its households |
| 5 | holdings | a village, Castleton, a keep, a barab season-camp |
| 4 | grounds | a farmstead, a mill, a market ground, a mine-head |
| 3 | places | a house, a crossing, a bubble's SCENE sits here |
| 2 | spots | the door, the hearth, the reed-cut |
| 1–0 | fine grain | positions within a spot; the beat-scale stage |

`temporal:urb` (floor 10, append-mode): the chronicles pour in as sediment — epochs
at the top rungs, the Coming Periods as forward-facing voicing at the world rung
(intention arrives in every read for free). `identity:urb` (registered): the glossary
races and orders at their scale-matched rungs — cultures high, families at region,
individuals at place.

## Seeding plan (order matters — top rungs outrank everything)

1. World rung + great divisions (pscale 10–9): the conditionality facts, from the
   glossary + David's canon (NOTE: gipos exist only in conversation — first write
   gives them their first address). Hours of work; fences every seat and every
   future mint at once.
2. Regions (8) from the geography; the Godway as linear region.
3. `temporal:urb` poured from the chronicles (epochs → eras → events, verbatim-close,
   sedimented). `identity:urb` from the glossary. `keeper:urb` seeded from the
   almanac (its GM Notes are keeper content), including play-guidance registers
   (mood, themes, how the world answers).
4. `mint:gal` gains an authoring-time fence: read the spine's high rungs before
   rolling; the placing stub names a skeleton coordinate. Encounter-ladder horse
   slots re-grounded (orini / the unbroken orin).
5. Bubble envelope delivery: the door arrives with ancestor underscores of its
   placing coordinate riding above the scene — the horizon, by inheritance, no
   separate block.
