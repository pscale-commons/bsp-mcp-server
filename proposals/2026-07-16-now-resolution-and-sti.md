## 0. The non-multiplication commitment

Pscale blocks fold by self-similar shapes; the failure mode is components. This design
adds **zero new primitives, zero new block families, zero daemons, zero clocks-as-state**.
Everything below is: directive gates (judgment over relations the envelope already
renders), per-world declarations in the rules block that already exists, and the *naming*
of patterns the substrate already runs so future sessions stop re-deriving them. The three
use-cases it serves, in one sentence each:

- **LLM engagement** — the temporal grounding gives every reader its ages pre-rendered;
  an LLM never subtracts (the rungs are the LLM's vocabulary; humans get "6h ago" —
  two audiences, two phrasings, deliberately not aligned).
- **Real-world coordination (bleeding-edge now)** — people state intentions at the day's
  grain and the fold meets them when now catches up; earth is wall-coupled by nature.
- **RPG** — the equivalent psycho-social dimensional space, run with a mutable now or,
  later, against settled canon (block universe). Same geometry, one sign.

## 2. The three gates of coarse resolution

A window at a coarse room (`pool:3200`, the town at +2) closes only when ALL three hold:

1. **Time has arrived** — now has caught up with the window's span. Mechanics: none.
   The grounding already renders the window's open-stamp with its age at rung
   ("opened … (+2 — days ago)"); the determiner READS dueness — a +2 window whose stamp
   is a day old is due; no arithmetic, no scheduler. Enforcement is stigmergic: the next
   reader after due folds (or stands down); the crab (worktable 2.7) makes it prompt when
   nobody is looking. Late is fine — narrative time is elastic, and nothing finer can
   contradict a fold whose finer events were absorbed.
2. **The finer life beneath is absorbed** — built (#150): the envelope lists live finer
   windows; the directive holds the fold open until they resolve, and the coarse fold
   absorbs their outcomes as its fine detail. Refinement (a read, not a mechanism): hand
   the determiner the finer pools' committed record for the window's interval, not only
   the live windows.
3. **A determiner claims it** — built: the store's single-resolution claim
   (`resolves_window`) admits exactly one fold at any pool.

Influence flows both ways around the gates, and both directions already have carriers:
**downward during** (a coarse intention is Background weather in every fine room beneath —
built) and **upward at close** (the fold absorbs the interval — gate 2).

## 3. Exposure follows intention (the AFK law, generalised)

The beat-grain safety rule ("an ABOUT character cannot be contested") is the pscale-0
case of the general law: **a character is present at the grain of their standing
intention, and can be met — and can die — at that grain.** Nobody duels you at a table
you are not at; a week-grain intention ("climb across the mountains") walks you through
week-grain events, and if the only pass is the one with bandits, geometry pulls you in.
*You are as safe as your last intention is boring.* A parked character with no intention
drifts at the day's grain, exposed to nothing.

- **Geometry outranks disposition.** The forced intersection (one pass) includes you
  regardless. The *bend-toward-inclusion vs play-it-safe* parameter is a *disposition* in
  the world's rules block tuning only the ambiguous middle — soft by design.
- **Returning is honest.** The fold writes the public record (grit's bite rule already
  demands consequences that bite); the player returns to a world that moved, possibly to
  a character who died at the pass.
- **The body-moving seam (open, convention needed before the first death):** the
  determiner writes the PUBLIC record only; a character's own blocks stay with their
  keeper. Death as a public fact the next perceive meets is clean; a determiner rewriting
  a dead character's `passport:3` is not. Settle this at the worktable before a world
  runs lethal coarse folds.

## 4. The now-advance dial (per-world, one declaration)

Does now advance by wall-clock or by attention? Both are legitimate worlds; the
declaration sits in the **rules block, beside the ladder** — the same place dice and the
grain deviation live.

- **Wall-coupled** (earth by nature; kind to returning players): fictional time tracks
  real time; the grounding's rendered ages ARE fictional dueness; the week-away climber's
  week passes in real weeks.
- **Attention-elastic** (a table playing fast): now advances as fast as the coarsest
  quiet fold allows — everyone at +2 with intentions staged folds the day in ten wall
  minutes. The grounding's ages then read as *attention-staleness* (the lender's and
  ear's time), and fictional dueness is the fold's judgment. The intention's own span is
  the natural floor either way — a week-intention is not foldable by an eager afternoon.

## 5. The XYZ settings are the sign of the time ahead of now

In sunstone's block-sign vocabulary: **bleeding-edge-now = the future is minus** —
mutable, liquid, gathered-not-settled; intentions are proposals the fold weaves.
**Block universe = the future is plus** — settled canon (imported from a book, or played
elsewhere); fine play interweaves and reads canon as constraint, never altering it.
Same geometry, one sign.

- **Minus (now): feasible immediately** — it is the merged machinery plus the gates of §2.
- **Plus (block universe): deferred, path named, no new geometry** — canon lands as
  deposited events on the sundial skeleton (`canon:<world>` borrowing Gregorian
  addresses, each entry carrying its spatial ref), plus the read-only-constraint
  discipline in the directive. Defer until a world needs it; nothing about the beach
  blocks it — the biome's aspect law is the recipe.
