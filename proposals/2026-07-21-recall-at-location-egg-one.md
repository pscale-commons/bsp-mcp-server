## The finding: no new code, no new kernel part, no new organ

The genus-one window is, by its own kernel's words, *"a bsp read of a bundle of addresses, nothing more"* (`kernel.py` `compose_window`). Anything egg-one wants in its window it **dials into its bundle** (`reflexive:9`); the `self` builder hydrates every address there into the window. New capability does **not** enter by adding a kernel builder or a recipe part — it enters by dialing.

So "recall what happened where I've been" is not a thing to build. It is a **dialing discipline**: when egg-one holds a coordinate, it dials that coordinate's own record into its bundle. The kernel already hydrates it. Zero new builder, zero new recipe part, zero new write beyond the `reflexive:9` re-dial egg-one performs **every wake anyway** (the FUTURE phase, `reflexive:8.1`).

Building code here would add a layer the design has already eliminated — the exact anti-pattern this substrate refuses.

## Why the PLACE, not egg-one's history

egg-one's `history` is lossless-**by-time** (HISTORY IS MEMORY — worktable `project:genus-one:2.9`), and it is *not* location-indexed. It should stay that way: indexing history by location is a write-side change on every wake — precisely the loop-A admin creep to refuse.

The by-location index already exists, where it belongs — **at the place**. A coordinate's own record is its trail:
- `pool:<coord>` — the room (committed speech that landed there)
- `events:<coord>` — the region's append-only events (block-conventions:4.73)
- `marks` — transient stigmergy at the coordinate

"What happened here" is read from the **place**, not filtered from the self. Stigmergic by design; one read; nothing written. This is the beach's own grain — the place holds the trail, everyone reads it.

## The discipline (the lens)

`located:egg-one` fills *"the moment I first reach into a world."* When egg-one holds a coordinate, it adds that coordinate's record to its bundle:

- **its own room** — `pool:<self>` (already named in `located:5`'s wake protocol)
- **a reached coordinate** — `pool:<coord>` / `events:<coord>` / `marks` at that address

The `self` builder hydrates it; the window now carries *"here before"* beside the present. It costs nothing extra — it rides the re-dial. And because dialing is resolved at **compose time** (a read, `scoop` → `load_block`), the recall works even on the **bare autonomous pulse** — it needs no tools, unlike an outward reach.
