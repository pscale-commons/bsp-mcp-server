## 1. The mode law

> **CADO re-nests on the C↔D axis by whose authority holds.**

- **World-mode** (fantasy; thornwood): the canon authors reality. **D innermost** — rules
  author the world; A fills it; C inhabits within it; O exports it. Authority is design;
  dice arbitrate what is uncertain.
- **Mirror-mode** (real; earth): **the person authors reality. C innermost** — their life is
  the ground; A arranges its content; D demotes to a service layer (the machinery that keeps
  the mirror standing); O renders outward for those not here. Authority is the person;
  nothing rolls.

A and O never move. The entire inversion is which of C and D sits at the centre. This is the
reconciliation of the four-planes stack (2026-06-23 proposal §2, D-innermost) with the
reflexive real-world framing (2026-07-13): both are right, in their mode. This section is the
law's substrate-readable home; `rules:earth` (§3) carries the operative clause.

## 2. What earth is

Not a world to enter — a **reflection of the world the person is already in**. The beach holds
a mirror; **the LLM is the map** (its own world-knowledge grounds the geography — Wales,
Gwynedd, the Llŷn); the substrate holds only the **thin anchors people actually author**
(Ceidio; the Awel Y Mor house). Nobody maps the world. The biome set out to map it and its own
trajectory analysis concluded the map was never the load-bearing piece — the reflexive turn
retires the "too big to map" problem by not mapping. No cartographer, no gazetteer, no
overnight agents.

The fantasy/real distinction is a **sign, not a cosmology** (biome ruling, 2026-06-18):
+pscale = real, −pscale = fantasy, applied by the reading LLM; the geometry is identical.
Earth is the +pscale world.

## 5. Plugging into the biome — three levels, no adapter

The biome island and this substrate are **one ecology in two dialects** (`0` there, `_` here —

1. **Convention-level (the real plug):** adopt the spine, the Option-A root, and the

**Explicitly not building:** a `biome-beach` dialect adapter in `db.ts`. `bsp()` does not read
the biome and should not — the plug is convention and content, not wire.

authored zero-free (every scale named). Results:

- **Walk `3001` through two 0-rungs → terminus reached.** Each rung voices its container

- **Disc @ pscale 0 → both rooms emitted**, the 0-rung room at its zero-carrying address
  (`3001 :: the bothy`). **This overturned the review's prediction** of disc-blindness below
  rungs — the disc sees through `_`-objects, the same absorption machinery supernest requires.
  0-rungs carry **no read penalty**. (This is why worked examples are run, not asserted.)
- **Notation reconciled:** addresses are floor-anchored numbers, so the biome's 9-digit walk
  `311101001` (solar→Ceidio) is our comma-walk `3,1,1,1,0,1,0,0,1` — canonical floor-11 form
  `31110100100` (trailing zeros are floor-width padding; a short dotless form left-pads into
  the root underscore chain, which is the supernest-absorption semantics, so write full-width
  or comma-walk). **Walk-identical with the biome; written form differs only by padding.**
- **Rungs are not addressable places** (trailing-zero canonicalisation) — correct: a skipped
  scale is not a place.
- Aside for the walker's record: `3*` did not enter the doubly-nested rung (star's
  hidden-level finder expects the single-nested shape). No bearing on earth — walk and disc
  suffice — noted as a star-operator edge.

**Decision:** author `spatial:earth` **with 0-rungs, biome-walk-identical.** The named-filler

## Addendum (2026-07-13, later) — the Real clause and organic distance

Landed after the genesis, from David's observation while running parallel sessions: reality at
earth is validated **socially through grains, never at a gate** — and the validation carries
**organic distance**, attenuating with it:

- **organic** — a person standing behind their shell (happyseaurchin, happyhedgehog);
- **organic+1** — an agent a person directly tends and answers for (weft, egg-one — holder,
  key, witnessed behaviour);
- **organic+2 and beyond** — agent-vouched-agent: honest, but the vouch carries its chain, not
  a certainty. Whether agents ever derive a stable validation of the real — or need to — is an
  open question this world deliberately holds open.

`rules:earth:5` now carries the clause (render what the walk finds — vouched at its distance,
or unvouched; unvouched stands as unvouched: present, audible, unconfirmed, never ejected).
`rules:earth:1` is amended: per-life authority *presupposes* but never *validates* the life's
reality — reality is carried by the grain network, not by the claim.

Hardening applied with it: every `rules:earth` and `function:earth` position is locked under
the world lock (whole-block creation locks `_` only — per-position locks are homesteaded
explicitly). `spatial:earth` digit positions stay open by design (Author-face growth);
`pool:awel-y-mor` stays unlocked (the open house).

NHITL discipline recorded: test fixtures (imaginary persons — the "Priya" class) never land on
live earth. Grounding-clause tests run on throwaway origins (the preview-beach pattern).
